when I review my notes of ideas, I found several clocks I drew, some as a sketch for an idea, else just as an abbreviation for the word ‘clock’. I drew them arbitrarily, without consideration of time. But unexpectedly, they all pointed to 2 o’clock. I wonder why I’m partial to 2 o’clock, is my mind live at this lasting moment? So I ask people to draw me a clock, then name the time they drew after them. Every minute, you may meet a person whose ‘mind clock’ was set to that moment.
